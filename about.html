About

Nat Atnafu is an Engineer and Artist living in Somerville, MA.

Nat blends technology and creativity into interactive electronic art. Drawing on his understanding of electrical systems, his installations combine sensors, light, touch, and motion to bring viewers into an experience. His work touches on ideas of human connection, self growth and exploration, nature, and technology.
