Mindshark LED Dome

2016 – 2025 · Burning Man

A 32-foot interactive dome featuring over 7,000 LEDs — audio reactive and controllable via paintbrush "wands".

Installed every year at Burning Man from 2016 through 2025.

Collaboration with Ashoat Tevosyan, Larry Fenn, and Camp Mindshark.
