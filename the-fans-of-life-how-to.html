The Fans of Life — How to Interact

To start a fan, spin it with your finger.

To stop a fan, stop it with your finger.

Watch the fans change as they play out the Game of Life.

Each fan has two states: alive or dead (on or off).

Every 2 seconds, the fans change state based on the following rules:

  • A live fan survives if it has 2–3 living neighbors and dies otherwise.
  • A dead fan comes to life if it has 3 living neighbors.

Changing a fan with your finger will reset the 2 second timer, allowing you to edit multiple fans before the next change.

These simple rules can create extremely complex patterns.

Life is unpredictable, so once in a while a random fan will turn on!
