Remote Touch

2021

Remote Touch is an interactive piece that connects two people through the sensation of warmth, regardless of the distance between them.

The work consists of two framed copper panels, each embedded with electronics. When someone places their hand on one panel, the other heats up in response — and cools when they pull away. Through this simple exchange, two people can feel each other's presence and share a moment together while apart.

Remote Touch was chosen to be a Connection Station at the show Belonging by Castle Of Our Skins on 11/5/2023.
