Resistance is a force that shows up when you try to make a change.

It will do everything in its power to keep things as they are.

Sometimes, it takes a lot of power to OVERCOME Resistance.

Eight letters created using electrical resistors. 120V was applied to each letter, resulting in 1800 watts of power to overload the resistors.
